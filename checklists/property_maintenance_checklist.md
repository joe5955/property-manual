# Property Maintenance Checklist
*Shady Cove Estate — Recurring Maintenance Tasks*

This checklist consolidates all maintenance tasks identified in the property manual. Use it to schedule and track recurring inspections, servicing, and replacements across all buildings and systems.

---

## Monthly Tasks

### Electrical & Safety
- [ ] Test all GFCI outlets (especially waterfront/dock locations)
- [ ] Inspect boat ramp panel for moisture intrusion and rust progression
- [ ] Verify all panel doors are closed and latched

### Water Systems
- [ ] Check water tank levels and pressure readings
- [ ] Inspect well pump operation and pressure gauge
- [ ] Check for visible leaks at all shutoff valves

### General
- [ ] Walk all buildings and check for signs of water intrusion, pest activity, or structural issues
- [ ] Check fire extinguisher pressure indicators

---

## Quarterly Tasks

### Heating Systems (Main House)
- [ ] Replace furnace filters — Side Entry furnace (Thermo Pride SSO-100)
- [ ] Replace furnace filters — Basement furnace (Thermo Pride, bedroom zone)
- [ ] Inspect flue pipes on both furnaces for blockage or damage
- [ ] Test both heating zones independently (living room zone and bedroom zone)

### Water Heaters
- [ ] Test pressure relief valve — Main House Side Entry water heater (Bradford White ERE350S6-1NCWW)
- [ ] Test pressure relief valve — Main House Basement water heater (Bradford White ERE250T6-1NCWW)
- [ ] Test pressure relief valve — Camp Kitchen Rinnai tankless water heater
- [ ] Test pressure relief valve — Beach House water heater
- [ ] Inspect all water heater connections for leaks

### Electrical
- [ ] Inspect all waterfront electrical connections for corrosion (dock panels, boat ramp panel)
- [ ] Test GFCI protection at all outdoor and marine outlets

### Thermostat
- [ ] Verify both Honeywell thermostats respond correctly to their respective furnace zones
- [ ] Check thermostat battery level (replace if low)

---

## Semi-Annual Tasks (Spring & Fall)

### Heating Oil System
- [ ] Monitor oil tank level — schedule delivery before heating season
- [ ] Check for oil leaks around both furnaces

### Generator (Dayton Diesel — Camp Kitchen)
- [ ] Change engine oil
- [ ] Inspect fuel system for leaks
- [ ] Test run under load
- [ ] Check coolant level and condition
- [ ] Inspect air filter

### Irrigation System (Main House — Rain Bird ESP-ME3)
- [ ] Test all irrigation zones
- [ ] Inspect sprinkler heads for damage or clogging
- [ ] Winterize system before first freeze (fall)
- [ ] Restart and test all zones after winter (spring)

### Boat House / Dock
- [ ] Inspect dock planks and hardware for rot, rust, or damage
- [ ] Check boat winch cables and mechanisms
- [ ] Inspect all dock electrical for corrosion
