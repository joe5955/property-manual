# Upper Shop & Associated Outbuildings

**Last Updated:** February 25, 2026

This document provides an overview of the Upper Shop building and the associated outbuildings located in its vicinity, including fuel storage, the booster pump shed, and storage sheds.

---

## 1. Upper Shop Building

The Upper Shop is a large, open workshop with a high ceiling, concrete floor, and a second-level loft. It is the primary maintenance and fabrication facility on the property.

### Exterior
- **[01_upper_shop_exterior.jpg](./client/public/images/upper-shop/exterior/01_upper_shop_exterior.jpg):** Exterior view of the large, dark-stained board-and-batten building with a gable roof and sliding barn-style doors.

### Interior
- **[01_upper_shop_interior.jpg](./client/public/images/upper-shop/interior/01_upper_shop_interior.jpg):** Interior view of the large, open workshop space. Equipment visible includes a table saw, bench grinder, air compressor, engine hoist, and pallet fork attachment.

### Electrical

## 2. Associated Outbuildings

### Fuel Storage (Property-Wide)
Located near the upper shop, these tanks serve the entire property.
- **[01_fuel_tanks_overview.jpg](./client/public/images/upper-shop/fuel-storage/01_fuel_tanks_overview.jpg):** Two elevated fuel tanks for Unleaded Gasoline (left) and Diesel (right) with gravity-feed dispensing.
- **[02_fuel_tank_regular.jpg](./client/public/images/upper-shop/fuel-storage/02_fuel_tank_regular.jpg):** A third, separate tank for Regular (leaded) gasoline, also elevated on a frame.

### Booster Pump Shed (Serves Madrona House)
This shed is located behind the upper shop.
- **[01_booster_pump_shed_exterior.jpg](./client/public/images/upper-shop/booster-pump-shed/01_booster_pump_shed_exterior.jpg):** Exterior view of the small, dark-stained shed with a flat roof.
- **[02_booster_pump_shed_interior.jpg](./client/public/images/upper-shop/booster-pump-shed/02_booster_pump_shed_interior.jpg):** Interior view showing the two booster pumps that serve the Madrona house. The blue pump is for irrigation water, and the green pump is for potable water. Both are controlled by Littelfuse PumpSaver units.

### Storage Sheds
Two matching sheds are located behind the upper shop.
- **[01_storage_sheds.jpg](./client/public/images/upper-shop/storage-sheds/01_storage_sheds.jpg):** The two weathered gray storage sheds. The left shed is for general storage, and the right shed is for paint storage.
