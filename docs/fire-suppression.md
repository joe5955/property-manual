# Property-Wide Fire Suppression System

**Last Updated:** February 25, 2026

This document details the property's dedicated fire suppression system, a key safety feature and a requirement for insurance coverage.

---

## 1. System Overview

The system is designed to provide a high-volume water supply for firefighting purposes anywhere on the property. It consists of a large water reservoir and a strategically located fire hydrant.

- **Insurance Requirement:** This system was installed to meet specific requirements for property insurance.
- **Water Source:** A 10,000-gallon dedicated water reservoir tank.
- **Distribution:** A 6-inch underground supply line runs from the reservoir to the fire hydrant.
- **Access Point:** A 6-inch galvanized fire hydrant standpipe.
