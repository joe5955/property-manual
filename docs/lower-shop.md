# Lower Shop

**Last Updated:** February 25, 2026

This document provides an overview of the Lower Shop, a large workshop building with extensive woodworking equipment, pond pump controls, EV charging, and multiple electrical panels and phase converters. The building is divided into three main sections: South Side, North End, and West End.

---

## 1. South Side

The south side of the Lower Shop features a large sliding door, a covered carport/bay, and serves as a general workshop and storage area.

- **[01_exterior_south.jpg](./client/public/images/lower-shop/south-side/01_exterior_south.jpg):** Exterior view showing the large sliding door, covered carport with a silver SUV, and a John Deere tractor parked outside.
- **[02_interior_south.jpg](./client/public/images/lower-shop/south-side/02_interior_south.jpg):** Interior view showing the loft with storage, a motorcycle/scooter, and general workshop clutter.
- **[03_electrical_area.jpg](./client/public/images/lower-shop/south-side/03_electrical_area.jpg):** The main electrical area, including the closed main breaker panel, pond pump pressure switch, and tool storage.
- **[04_panel_south.jpg](./client/public/images/lower-shop/south-side/04_panel_south.jpg):** The Square D QO Load Center (Cat No. QOC30U) labeled "Pond Panel," which serves as the service disconnect.

## 3. West End

The west end of the shop is dedicated to heavy-duty, 3-phase woodworking machinery.

- **[01_woodshop_west.jpg](./client/public/images/lower-shop/west-end/01_woodshop_west.jpg):** The west end woodshop area, containing large green industrial machinery such as a planer/jointer, wide drum sander, and bandsaw.
- **[02_panel_west.jpg](./client/public/images/lower-shop/west-end/02_panel_west.jpg):** The Square D QO Load Center for the west end, with its own service disconnect.
- **[03_ronk_phase_converter.jpg](./client/public/images/lower-shop/west-end/03_ronk_phase_converter.jpg):** A Ronk Auto-Transformer / Phase Converter mounted on the wall, providing 3-phase power.
- **[04_safety_disconnect.jpg](./client/public/images/lower-shop/west-end/04_safety_disconnect.jpg):** A Square D General Duty Safety Switch and various outlets near the doorway to the north end.
