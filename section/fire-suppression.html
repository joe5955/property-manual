Fire Suppression — Overview

System Overview

Insurance Requirement: This system is a requirement for property insurance. System Components:
  • 10,000-gallon dedicated water reservoir tank.
  • 6-inch underground supply line.
  • 6-inch galvanized fire hydrant standpipe.
6-inch galvanized fire hydrant standpipe with elbow fitting, on a concrete/gravel pad with access hatch. Located on gravel drive area among conifers.
10,000-gallon water reservoir tank for fire suppression. Large dark horizontal cylindrical tank elevated on steel/concrete cradle supports among the trees.
