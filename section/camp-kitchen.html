Generator Switchover Instructions (Transcribed from Handwritten Note)

Steps to Switch to Generator Power:
  • Start generator
  • Turn off House Main Switch #1
  • Turn off main switch on panel #2
  • Switch #3 Transfer Switch to Gen.Set
  • Turn on House Main Switch #1
  • Turn on main switch on panel #2
To Switch Back to OPALCO Power:
  • Turn off House Main Switch #1
  • Turn off main switch on panel #2
  • Switch #3 Transfer Switch to OPALCO
  • Turn off generator
  • Turn on House Main Switch #1
  • Turn on main switch on panel #2

Maintenance Notes

  • Winterization: Shut off water supply and drain lines to prevent freezing.
  • Refrigerator: Amana TX18VW is from 1997 — replacement strongly recommended.
  • Water Heater: Rinnai R75LS set at 135°F — verify temperature setting periodically.
