Electrical & Transfer Switch

Power Switchover — To Generator:
  • Turn on OPALCO flood light
  • Turn main switch to OFF
  • Start generator — warm up
  • Turn main switch to GENSET
Power Switchover — To OPALCO:
  • Turn main switch to OFF
  • Turn off generator
  • Turn main switch to OPALCO
  • Turn off OPALCO flood light
Transfer Switch: Square D manual safety switch, 3-position (GENSET / OFF / OPALCO) Distribution Panel: Square D QO Load Center serving Beach House, Farm House, Caretaker's House, and sub-panel circuits. Sub-Panel: Square D QO2-4L70S — View Manual, 70A, 240V, Single Phase. Labeled 'CHARGE ONLY / PLUGS'.

Square D safety switch / manual transfer switch. 3-position handle: GENSET (top), OFF (middle), OPALCO (bottom). Square D Company label with DANGER warning.
