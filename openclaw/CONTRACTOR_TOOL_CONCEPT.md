# AI Tool Concept: The "Digital Jobsite" for General Contractors

## The Core Concept
A plug-and-play, AI-powered system designed specifically for General Contractors to manage bids, track costs, and document the physical reality of a build using QR codes and photos. It turns a chaotic jobsite into an organized, searchable digital record while automating the math of bidding.

## Key Features

**1. The "X-Ray" QR Code System (As-Built Documentation)**
- Physical QR code stickers are placed on framing, utility rough-ins, and appliance locations *before* drywall goes up.
- Contractors snap a photo of the framing/wiring/plumbing and link it to the QR code.
- Years later, anyone can scan the wall or appliance location to see exactly what is behind the drywall (pipes, wires, studs) or access the manual for that specific appliance.

**2. Plug-and-Play Bidding Engine**
- A standardized module for estimating costs.
- The contractor inputs their baseline metrics: framing material costs, employee hourly rates, subcontractor bids, and estimated man-hours per phase.
- The AI engine calculates the total bid, factoring in margins and overhead, creating a professional, ready-to-send proposal.

**3. Modular Design**
- The QR documentation and the Bidding Engine are treated as separate but integrated modules. A contractor can use one or both, depending on their needs.
